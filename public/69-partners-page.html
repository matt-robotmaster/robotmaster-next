Please review the following new V6.9 updates and the most recent enhancements. While Robotmaster drives innovations within the V7 platform, the technical team continues to ensure strong support of the V6 product line. Please become familiar with all the new features, improvements, post processor enhancements, and bug fixes in V6.9.

What's new in Robotmaster V6.9

  • Mastercam 2021 Adapter
  • Toolkit Activator to create cell and tooling setup 
  • Refined tool management between Mastercam and Robotmaster 
    • Extended one to one mapping of the cutting and non-cutting part of geometries

Supported Mastercam Versions 

  • Mastercam 2021
  • Mastercam 2017, 2018, 2019, 2020
  • Mastercam X9
    • Note: We recommend using Mastercam 2017 - 2021

Improvements 

  • Localization of Russian language
  • Localization of Portuguese language

Bugs Fixed

  • Fixed a major bug that occurred when Importing custom tool and holders
  • Staubli FTP Transfer
  • Inconsistencies in the localization of the repose tab
Installation Instructions
  • It is very important to backup your Robotmaster shared files before downloading and installing the update. For further instructions on how to back up your files, refer to How to back up my Robotmaster files
  • Remove any previously installed versions of Robotmaster V6.2 - 6.5. If you have Robotmaster V6.6, V6.7 or V6.8, Robotmaster V6.9 will overwrite it.
  • Remove any previously installed versions of the Robotmaster toolkit prior to installing Robotmaster V6.9.
  • For detailed installation instructions, please refer to How to install.
Please Note: Robotmaster V6.9 cannot be installed side by side with any previous versions of Robotmaster V6.2, V6.3, V6.4, V6.5, V6.6, V6.7 or V6.8.
Maintenance Date

Robotmaster V6.9 is supported for those customers who are under maintenance from March 01st, 2020 and onward.
Post Processors

Customized post processors from 6.0 and older are not compatible with 6.9. An update is required if it has been customized (please contact your distributor for any questions).

Customized post processors from 6.1 to 6.8 are compatible with 6.9 and X9, but require a quick update to fully support Mastercam 2017, 2018, 2019, 2020, and 2021

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Here are a few FAQs that we seem to be getting a lot in regards to V6 and V7. If you would like to add a question that you receive a lot and you think it needs to be addressed, please email us back at feedback@robotmaster.com with your question or comments.
 

  • I keep hearing about Robotmaster V7, what is the difference between Robotmaster Version 6 and Version 7?
    • Robotmaster V7 is more than a new version, it is an entirely new product line. Robotmaster V7 is a new standalone product which was built on a completely redesigned architecture to help with the growing demands for robotic programming.

 

  • Will Robotmaster V7 be replacing Robotmaster V6?
    • Yes, V7 will eventually replace V6. The V7 edition that will replace V6 products is the Robotmaster Productivity suite (Productivity and Productivity Pro).

 

  • If I am on active maintenance, can I upgrade to Robotmaster Version 7?
    • We are currently creating a Mastercam to Robotmaster Interface for V7, which will be available in late-2020. Once the Mastercam Interface is available for V7, our existing customers that are on active maintenance will have the opportunity to switch to V7â€™s Productivity Suite. 
 
  • How long will you be supporting V6?
    • Robotmaster V6 will continue to be supported for a couple of years after the release of the Productivity Suite. We will be continuing to support the new Mastercam versions and adding some additional enhancements, bug fixes and features.
